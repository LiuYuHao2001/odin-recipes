Cripsy Pork Belly

Description

Crispy pork belly is all the rage, thanks to social media, food bloggers, and celebrity chefs with too much time on their hands. It's a common sight on menus across the land; you can't channel-surf past a food show without seeing it. It also helps that people are no longer terrified to eat a little fat.

Ingredients

  • 1kg pork belly with scored skin
  • 3 tbsp salt
  • 2 tsp white pepper

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Pat dry the skin and rub 2 tbsp salt and 2 tsp pepper over the pork. Place on a tray and into the fridge overnight, uncovered.
  2. The next day, remove pork from the fridge for at least 1 hr before roasting.
  3. Preheat oven to 180°C. Place pork on a rack. Pat dry the skin with paper towels. Rub the skin with remaining salt, ensuring salt gets into the scoring. Rub the skin with oil.
  4. Place the rack onto a tray and into the oven. Reduce temperature to 160°C and roast for 2½ hrs. Increase the temperature to 240°C and roast for another 30 mins or until skin has crackled.
  5. Remove from the oven and rest for 10 mins before cutting.
